Bonsai Website Design

Affordable Web Design for Small Businesses

Grow your local business and increase customer reach with a strong social media presence and mobile-friendly website.

Website Design

Generally the most costly aspect of the process, the design will usually cost between 600-1200 depending on the complexity. Email us to get a quote on your website at hugo@bonsaidesign.com.au.

Hosting

Hosting is how you store your website on the internet. Generally bought as monthly subscriptions, they're priced at around $4 a month.

We'll happily handle your hosting on our personal server at $4 a month if you don't have a hosting service already.

Domain Name

A domain name is what your website is called, like "www.google.com". They're generally bought in yearly subscriptions, and are usually about $20 per year.

We'll happily handle your domain subscription in addition to building your website at no extra cost (besides the cost of the domain of course).
