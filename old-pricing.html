Our Plans

We offer many options to best suit your needs. Whether you already have a domain and hosting service, or don't even know what those words mean, we're able to tailor a pricing plan just for you.
Domain Names A domain name is what your website is called, like "www.google.com". They're generally bought in yearly subscriptions, and are usually about $20 per year.

We'll happily handle your domain subscription in addition to building your website at no extra cost (besides the cost of the domain of course).
Hosting
Hosting is how you store your website on the internet. Generally bought as monthly subscriptions, they're generally around $4 a month.
We'll happily handle your hosting on our personal server at $4 a month if you don't have a hosting service already.
Website Design
Generally the most costly aspect of the process, the design will usually cost between 600-1200 depending on the complexity. Email us to get a quote on your website at hugo@bonsaidesign.com.au.
